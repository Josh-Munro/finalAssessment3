Nepal blog

11/10/2019

The Nepal trip was a amazing experience. It was the first time I had visited the Himalayas. I visited the red pandas in Kathmandu. These fury creatures were a marvel to look at. With there fluffy red fur circlign their cheecky faces. The Red Pandas were sitting on the tall bamboo branches in the mountains. The Pandas seem to master the skill of resting on the most perculiar places. The bamboo are tall and thin, yet the Red Pandas are able to comfortably sleep up there all day.
