|About me

Robert Davis

Hello my name is Rob Davis I am New Zealander who lives in sunny Nelson. I have a true passion for nature and is fortunate to have travelled to so many places to enable me to take amazing photos of earth's nature.
I have a wife and two kids "Josh" and "Kate".

I have studied Ethology at the university of Otogo for 3 years. photogrpahy grew as a interest, then a hobby, until it turned into a passion that I commit alot of my life to.

My Passions

I have a passion for photogrpahy and nature. So why not combine them together to capture beautiful nature all around the world. I have a passion for devleoping my photogrpahy skills and challenging myself to find wildlife in the most dangeorus places.

Where I've been

I've been to the Hymalias, Kathmandu, Asia, Australia, Iceland, Europe, Africa (South Africa, Kenya, Ghana), and of course New Zealand

My favourite place that I have been to is Africa. Africa was filled with culture and the craziess animals. I managed to capture the greatest mammals alive. Elephants, Lions, Hyenas, Hippos, ect.
